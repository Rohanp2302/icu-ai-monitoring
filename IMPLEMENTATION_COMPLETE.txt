THE MULTI-MODAL APPROACH EXPLAINED SIMPLY
==========================================

Why Dual Paths (DL + ML)?

Deep Learning Path:
- Transformer model
- Looks at TIME PATTERNS
- Sees hour-by-hour changes
- Good at: Detecting deterioration OVER TIME
- Weakness: Needs lots of temporal data

Machine Learning Path:
- Random Forest
- Looks at FEATURE RELATIONSHIPS
- Sees which vitals matter most together
- Good at: Understanding which vitals combinations are dangerous
- Weakness: Doesn't see time patterns well

Combined (Ensemble):
- DL catches temporal degradation ("patient getting worse")
- ML catches feature importance ("these specific vitals together = danger")
- Agreement scoring: When both agree → confidence HIGH
- When they disagree → flag as uncertain, needs review
- Result: Better than either alone

4 Validation Layers:
- Layer 1: Do models agree? (catch over-confidence)
- Layer 2: Does prediction make medical sense? (catch missed dangers)
- Layer 3: Is prediction like other similar patients? (catch outliers)
- Layer 4: Is prediction time-consistent? (catch sudden unexplained changes)

Effect: False Negatives AND False Positives prevented
